About Me

A Little Intro

Born in Leicester, raised in Leicester, hoping not to die in Leicester. I've loved music since playing keyboard at age 8, all the way to the church organs and concert grands I play now. Along the way I've picked up few extras of course, including double bass, trombone, and more. Recently I've gained an interest in web development, hence this poorly coded project of mine. It's taken up plenty of my evenings, but every second was worth it.

Where I'm based

As I mentioned before I'm Leicester based. So for the crazy few of you who don't know where that is, it's in the centre of the midlands, 1 hour south of Nottigham.

Where I study

Currently I'm an upper 6th form student of Wigston College! Here I study Music, Music Technology, and Computer Science. And when I'm not studying, I'm either chilling, or performing!
